Philippines

The Philippines,a country located in the Pacific Ocean, is a country made up of over 7,000 beautiful islands. Each island has its own special charm, making it a wonderful destination for travelers who want to experience adventure, relaxation, and culture. With its natural beauty.

Baguio


Baguio, located in the Mountains of the Philippines and is known as the country's summer capital because of its cooler climate. Baguio offers amazing and never forgetting senic views,however make sure to bring a jacket because it is a very cold place.

Cebu


One of the rather popular places to visit in the Philippines, Cebu located Centural Visayas, is know for its photogenic and dream like looking beaches. Cebu is also known for its under the sea atractions. Cebu is usually called as "Queen City of the south" because it was one of the first capitals of the Philippines

Tagaytay


Due to its breathtaking beauty, Tagaytay is one of the most visited locations in the south. Given its high height, it views Taal Lake, the Taal Volcano, and the chilly weather. This allows for several vantage points to observe the volcano and lake.
